## Introduction
This dashboard was designed to help job seekers, career switchers, and professionals better understand the data job market. Information about data roles is often scattered across multiple platforms, making it difficult to see clear trends. Using a real world dataset of 2024 data related job postings including job titles, salaries, and locations, this dashboard brings everything together in one interactive view. It allows users to easily explore market trends, compare roles, and gain insights into compensation and demand across different regions.

## Skills Showcased
- **Analytical Measures and KPIs:** Developed measures to calculate business-relevant metrics such as median yearly salary and total job count.

- **Insight-Driven Visualizations:** Built clear and effective charts including column, bar, line, and area visuals to analyze trends and compare roles.

- **Geo spatial Reporting:** Created map visualizations to analyze the geographic distribution of data roles.

- **Reporting and KPI Presentation:** Designed cards and tables to present key metrics and enable detailed data exploration.

- **Dashboard Design and Storytelling:** Delivered an intuitive and professional dashboard layout focused on clear insight communication.

- **Interactive User Experience:** Implemented slicers, bookmarks, buttons, and drill-through functionality to support dynamic analysis and navigation.


## Dashboard Overview

### Page 1: High-Level Market View 

![Dashboard Page 1](/Images/project1_page1.png)

This page provides a high-level overview of the data job market. It highlights key metrics such as total job count, median salary, and the most in-demand job titles, allowing users to quickly understand current market trends. The visuals are designed to support fast insight discovery and informed decision-making at a glance.

### Page 2: Job Title Drill Through 

![Dashboard Page 1](/Images/project1_page2.png)

This page enables detailed analysis at the individual job title level. Users can drill through from the main dashboard to explore salary ranges, work-from-home availability, top hiring platforms, and geographic distribution of roles. This view supports deeper exploration and comparison of specific roles across regions and employment conditions.
